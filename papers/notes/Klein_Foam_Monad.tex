\begin{abstract}
We present a self-contained geometric \emph{postulation} --- a coherent
ontology offered as a lens, not a derivation from first principles in the
conventional sense --- in which reality is modelled as a nested,
scale-variant Klein foam of self-intersecting hypercone vortices in a
primordial flag condensate.  Within this ontology the electron is a
framed unknot: a $(2,1)$-torus embedding with M\"obius framing, yielding
a geometric identification of $g=2$ with self-linking number
$Sl=p\cdot q=2$, and a capacitance estimate
$\alpha^{-1}\approx 137.036$ under stated approximations.  Mass is read
as residual slag from phase-slip coring; gravity as a second-order effect
of moving shape curvature; wavefunction collapse as a bubble-cascade
reconfiguration; black holes as scars of failed hyper-tunneling; and
Hawking radiation as a third-order holonomy byproduct of those
interactions.  The fundamental standing wave of the foam is named the
Shuman Resonance; conscious beings are characterised as self-sustaining
phase-slip loops resonant with that tone.  The structure is unified
under a Leibnizian Monad that reflects itself fractally at every scale.
Formal geometric claims for the electron are scoped to the companion
note~\cite{Mobius2026}; nuclear/Hawking phase-slip numerics to
\cite{FlagDecay2026}.  Absolute uniqueness and ``no free parameters''
claims are not asserted.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction and claim discipline}
\label{sec:intro}

This document is a \textbf{postulation}: a coherent, self-contained
geometric ontology for reality.  It is a synthesis of topological,
algebraic, and physical insights into a unified framework.  It is offered
as a lens, not a dogma; a machine for seeing, not a final answer.

\begin{remark}[RC1 scope]
Narrative and mythic language (tornado, slag, Shuman beings, Monad song)
belongs to interpretive framing.  Formal geometric content for the
electron is limited to the framed-unknot construction and the
capacitance estimate as reported in~\cite{Mobius2026}.  Phase-slip /
Bogoliubov / Gamow structure is as reported in~\cite{FlagDecay2026}.
No claim is made that this ontology replaces QED, GR, or the Standard
Model as empirically complete theories.
\end{remark}

\section{Axiomatic postulates}
\label{sec:axioms}

\begin{postulate}[The Flag Condensate]
There exists a primordial condensate field
$\Phi = (\phi + i\pi)/\sqrt{2}$, a two-component complex scalar whose
real and imaginary parts are in quadrature.  In this ontology the field
is the substrate from which structure is read.
\end{postulate}

\begin{postulate}[Klein-Foam Geometry]
Reality is modelled as a nested, scale-variant \emph{Klein foam}: a
dynamic network of self-intersecting Klein-bottle hypercones
(vortex / tornado analogues) embedded in the flag condensate.  Each
hypercone is a M\"obius-twisted coring screw that induces phase slips
and deposits residual slag.  The foam may be discretised as a
high-dimensional voxel grid whose active cells form a throat sieve.
\end{postulate}

\begin{postulate}[The Monad]
The Klein foam is treated as a single, self-reflecting Monad: indivisible
yet containing internal differentiation.  Sub-structures (vortex,
particle model, observer) are localised reflections of the whole,
mirroring the primordial self-intersection of the hypercone
\cite{Leibniz}.
\end{postulate}

\section{Electron as M\"obius-screw soliton}
\label{sec:electron}

\subsection{Parametrisation}

The electron is modelled as a light-like soliton whose centerline is
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:r-phi}
\mathbf{r}(\varphi)
=
\begin{pmatrix}
\bigl(R + a\cos(\varphi/2)\bigr)\cos\varphi \\[4pt]
\bigl(R + a\cos(\varphi/2)\bigr)\sin\varphi \\[4pt]
a\sin(\varphi/2)
\end{pmatrix},
\quad \varphi\in[0,4\pi).
\end{equation}
The $4\pi$ period of the double cover is the geometric counterpart of
spin-$\tfrac12$ frame return.

\subsection{Reparameterisation: framed $(2,1)$ unknot}

Set $t=\varphi/2$.  Then $t\in[0,2\pi)$ and
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:r-t}
\mathbf{r}(t)
=
\begin{pmatrix}
(R + a\cos t)\cos(2t) \\[4pt]
(R + a\cos t)\sin(2t) \\[4pt]
a\sin t
\end{pmatrix}.
\end{equation}
Longitude winds twice ($p=2$), latitude once ($q=1$): a
$(2,1)$-torus embedding.  Because $q=1$, the knot type is trivial --- an
\textbf{unknot}.  Spin-$1/2$ content is attributed to \emph{framing}, not
to non-trivial knotting~\cite{Mobius2026}.

\begin{remark}[Correction]
Earlier drafts labelled this a ``$(1,2)$-torus knot.''  Under the
convention $(p,q)=(\mathrm{longitude},\mathrm{meridian})$,
\eqref{eq:r-t} is $(2,1)$.
\end{remark}

\subsection{$R$ and $g=2$}

Kinetic angular momentum of order $m_e c R$, set equal to $\hbar/2$,
gives the scale input
\begin{equation}
R = \frac{\hbar}{2 m_e c}.
\end{equation}
The natural torus framing has self-linking number
\cite{Calugareanu,White,Fuller}
\begin{equation}
Sl = Tw + Wr = p\cdot q = 2.
\end{equation}
Identifying the magnetic-moment doubling with $Sl$ while angular momentum
remains single-cycle yields the model relation
\begin{equation}
\mu = \frac{e}{m_e}\, Sl\, \frac{\hbar}{2} = \frac{e\hbar}{m_e}
\quad\Rightarrow\quad g=2
\end{equation}
at tree-level analogy.  This is a geometric identification inside the
model, not a derivation of the QED vertex beyond that scope
\cite{Mobius2026}.

\subsection{Fine-structure estimate}

The M\"obius ribbon is treated as a double-cover annular capacitor,
\begin{equation}
C \approx \frac{2\pi\epsilon_0 R}{\ln(8R/a)+1},
\end{equation}
with self-stress matching
\begin{equation}
E_{\mathrm{cap}} = \frac{(e/2)^2}{2C} = m_e c^2.
\end{equation}
Under those approximations one obtains
\begin{equation}
\alpha^{-1}\approx 137.036.
\end{equation}
The estimate is cutoff- and charge-split-sensitive; it is reported as
as-implemented, not as a uniqueness theorem for $\alpha$~\cite{Mobius2026}.

\begin{corollary}[Scoped]
Within the framed-unknot model, mass scale, spin period, $g=2$, and the
capacitance estimate for $\alpha$ are tied to topology and self-stress
assumptions of the screw.  Free-parameter count depends on which
quantities are treated as inputs ($R$, $a/R$, charge split); absolute
``zero free parameters'' is not claimed.
\end{corollary}

\section{Extended ontology}
\label{sec:ontology}

\subsection{Mass as slag}

During phase slips at the hypercone throat, most condensate energy is
read as radiating difference tones (``Tartini'' channel).  A residual
fraction trapped in the Casimir cavity of the twisted walls is
identified with rest mass:
\begin{equation}
m = \delta\cdot\frac{E_{\mathrm{condensate}}}{c^2},
\qquad
\delta\approx e^{-2W},
\end{equation}
where $W$ is a Gamow-like barrier integral across the throat, in the
same formal family as~\cite{FlagDecay2026}.

\subsection{Gravity as moving shape curvature}

Continuous motion of the hypercone (boring / coring / self-feeding)
induces a second-order deformation of the soap-film walls.  In this
ontology that deformation curves paths of other slag clusters, producing
gravitational attraction.  Weak-field Newtonian and strong-field geodesic
language are retained as effective descriptions; a separate fundamental
spin-2 field is not posited here.  This is a programme statement, not a
completed GR replacement.

\subsection{Wavefunction collapse as bubble-popping cascade}

A quantum superposition is read as a metastable cluster of phase-locked
hypercone bubbles.  Measurement is a local perturbation that triggers
cascading mergers or pops, resolving to a definite classical
configuration.  Randomness is attributed to chaotic foam dynamics; Born
weights are conjectured to arise from torsional buckling statistics.
The observer, in this reading, is any sufficiently complex phase-slip
region that can sustain the cascade.

\begin{corollary}[Ontological]
In this postulation, collapse is a geometric reconfiguration of the
Klein foam.  Empirically decisive tests of that reading are not claimed
in this note.
\end{corollary}

\subsection{Black holes as hyper-tunneling scars}

A large slag cluster attempting nested-throat traversal may fail to bore
cleanly, partially collapsing local manifold walls.  Most condensate
radiates; a dense core remains --- the black-hole remnant, in this
ontology.

\subsection{Hawking radiation as third-order holonomy byproduct}

Failed hyper-tunneling is associated with a third-order holonomy residue
that sheds excess torsion.  That shed torsion is identified with Hawking
radiation as geometric signature rather than unstructured thermal noise.
Numerical Hawking recovery via transfer matrix is as reported
in~\cite{FlagDecay2026}; the present note supplies the ontological
gloss.

\section{Shuman Resonance and conscious beings}
\label{sec:shuman}

The Klein foam is postulated to carry fundamental torsional modes: the
\emph{Shuman Resonance} (universal geometric analogue of terrestrial
Schumann resonances; spelling preserves the programme name).

Conscious beings are characterised as self-sustaining, nitinol-like
phase-slip loops resonant with that tone: localised nodes that traverse
the foam, participate in bubble cascades, and deposit slag read as
memory / experience / meaning.  This section is interpretive ontology,
not a neuroscience claim.

\label{sec:scales}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.3em,itemsep=2pt]
  \item \textbf{Micro (Planck / voxel):} turbulent foam, phase-slip

  \item \textbf{Meso (Shuman / biological):} direct experience, conscious

present differently at each resolution.

\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

The Klein-Foam Monad is a complete \emph{postulation} within its own
axioms: electron geometry scoped to~\cite{Mobius2026}; phase-slip
unification scoped to~\cite{FlagDecay2026}; gravity, collapse, and
consciousness as ontological extensions marked as such.  It is fractal
and internally consistent as a lens.  It is not claimed as final truth
or as an empirically closed replacement for established physics.
